How to Play

Basic Rules

Click on empty cells to block them and trap the cat. The cat will try to escape to the nearest edge.

Levels

Each level gets progressively harder with:

  • More initial blocks
  • Faster cat movement
  • Fewer moves allowed
  • Special cells (Level 6+)

Special Cells

In higher levels, you'll find special cells that can:

  • Remove a random block
  • Freeze the cat for 2 moves
  • Add an extra move
  • Clear adjacent cells
